Brawlhalla is a free-to-play fighting game developed by Blue Mammoth Games for Microsoft Windows, macOS, PlayStation 4, Xbox One and Nintendo Switch. The game was shown at PAX East in April 2014, and went into alpha later that month.

Bommie

Like most professional players, Boomie's interest in gaming started at a very young age, originally starting by using his sister's Nintendo DS. He began taking gaming more seriously starting with Mario Kart Wii, where he achieved the maximum rank at only 8 years of age.From there, Boomie transitioned to PC gaming, playing League of Legends and Rocket League, then eventually finding Brawlhalla after a friend's recommendation in late 2015. He traveled to his first event in 2016 where he placed 2nd, then afterwards, became near-unstoppable on LAN, never missing top 8 in 1v1s and only losing a single 2v2 tournament.
